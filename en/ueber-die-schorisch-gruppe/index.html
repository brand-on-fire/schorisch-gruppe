The SCHORISCH Group – Focusing on the Customer!

Introducing the SCHORISCH Group

Recognizing and meeting the requirements of our customers is the focus of our business activities. In us you have found a competent and flexible partner who wants to develop solutions with you and for you, to meet the challenges of today and tomorrow in a rapidly changing and digitized world. We invite you to discover this for yourself any time.

At the SCHORISCH Group, we stand for values such as strong relationships, clarity, creativity, continuity, and professionalism toward business partners and employees. The guiding principles that result from these values are enacted by us and will continue to determine our actions in future.

Focusing on the customer is not only a guiding theme for the SCHORISCH Group, but an everyday reality. This makes the company, now with around 140 employees, a valued partner for your customers for planning, engineering, and implementing challenging projects in two competence centers: electronic assemblies in Wentorf bei Hamburg as well as steel construction in Karstädt, Brandenburg.

History

2018Cooperation with AKKUSYS in the Security Technology business division of SCHORISCH Elektronik GmbH
2017Merger of SCHORISCH Intec and SCHORISCH Elektronik GmbH
2016Merger of SCHORISCH Systems and SCHORISCH Tralec to become SCHORISCH Elektronik GmbH
2015Closure of Haselund transformer construction and cooperation with company Schneefuß & Rhode or Schwamedico
2015Extensive investment into the hall / machinery / infrastructure at the Karstädt site
2015New construction of office buildings for sales & construction in Karstädt
2015Relocation from Reinbek to Wentorf bei Hamburg, expansion and investment into electronic assembly production
2014New owners Norbert Peter Sellner / Kirsten Schönharting
2013Change of board members and start of restructuring/reorientation of the SCHORISCH Group
